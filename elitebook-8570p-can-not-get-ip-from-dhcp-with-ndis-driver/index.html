EliteBook 8570p Can not get IP from DHCP with NDIS driver

Victor Ma
 2012-11-30  94 words   One minute    views  
Contents

Working on this issue for a few days now. Finally identified the problem is on the driver side, not the DHCP or PXE server side - all other computer works just fine.  Find online the this thread, and Symantec support also confirmed my finding.

It seems Intel has got a support case opened at Nov 8, 2012,  Please refer to here, but I can’t wait. Symantec Support suggest to use WinPE, I end up went this way.

This problem also have another symptom like 82579LM - DOS NDIS2-driver does not work - no ping response.
